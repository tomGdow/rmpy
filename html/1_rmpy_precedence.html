Footnotes
Exponentiation
  1. Exponentiation has right to left associativity. 2**3**2 is 29
  2. The power operator ** binds less tightly than an arithmetic or bitwise unary operator on its right, that is, 2**-1 is 0.5.
The % operator
The % operator is also used for string formatting; the same precedence applies
