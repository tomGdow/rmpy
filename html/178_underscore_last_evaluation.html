underscore and last result (interactive shell)
In many symbolic math systems, such as Matlab or Mathematica, you can use a variable like Ans or % to retrieve the last computed value.
Is there a similar facility in the Python shell?
  1. underscore (_)
  2. ipython takes this one step further and you can access every result with _ and its numeric value ( _1, for example
  3. IPython allows you to go beyond the single underscore _ with double (__) and triple underscore (___), returning results of the second- and third-to-last commands.
