Mime Type
Multipurpose Internet Mail Extension
Indicates the nature and format of a document
Syntax
  1. type/subtype
  2. type/subtype;parameter=value;

type is general category

subtype is exact type of data

Examples
  1. text/html
  2. text/plain;charset=UTF-8
  3. video/mp4
  4. audio/mpeg (mp3)
  5. image/png
  6. image/gif
  7. image/svg+xml
  8. video/quicktime(mov)
  9. application/pdf
  10. application/msword(Word)
  11. audio/x-wav(wav)
  12. application/octet-stream (generic binary data)
  13. <form action="http://localhost:8000/" method="post" enctype="multipart/form-data"> >
